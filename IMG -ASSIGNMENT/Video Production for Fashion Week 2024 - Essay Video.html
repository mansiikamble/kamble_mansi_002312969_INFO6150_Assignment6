Video Production for Fashion Week: NYFW 2024

Essay Video provided multicam live video and production for NYFW’s February 2024 shows.

Video Production for NYC’s Fashion Week 2024

Essay Video’s collaboration with iconic fashion brands such as Badgley Mischka, Brandon Maxwell, and Fforme allowed us to create visually stunning narratives that resonate with audiences worldwide. Our approach goes beyond traditional videography; we aim to encapsulate the emotion, the detailed craftsmanship, and the vibrant atmosphere that define these high-profile events. Through our lenses, we captured the essence of each brand, delivering content that reflects the sophistication and creativity inherent in their designs.

🔼 Watch highlights from 2024 NYFW shows featuring video production from Essay Video

At Essay Video, we understand that fashion is not just about clothing; it’s about storytelling. Each piece is a statement, each collection a narrative. Our team of experienced cinematographers, editors, and technicians worked closely with designers and event organizers to ensure that every shot, every angle, and every frame contributed to telling these stories in the most compelling way possible.

From the runway to the final cut, our commitment to excellence and innovation was evident in every aspect of our work. We provided a seamless blend of artistic vision and technical expertise, resulting in a product that not only met but exceeded our clients’ expectations.

Timelapse of the Brandon Maxwell Autumn Winter 2024 Collection

Innovative Display Solutions

In addition to our video production services, Essay Video was at the forefront of enhancing the visual experience at various shows with cutting-edge LED and display systems. We had the honor of installing immersive visual setups for Yves Saint Laurent, Dior, Bergdorf Goodman, and Puma. Our state-of-the-art displays provided the perfect backdrop, amplifying the impact of each collection and creating unforgettable moments for attendees.

LED cube provided by Essay Video for The Amazing Mostro show: Puma 2024

LED provided by Essay Video for Dior | Rouge 2024

Looking Forward

As we reflect on a successful month at NYFW, we are filled with gratitude for the opportunity to work with such remarkable clients and to contribute to the world of fashion in our unique way. Essay Video remains dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in video production and event presentation. We look forward to continuing to innovate and inspire at future fashion events and beyond.

For more information about our services or to discuss your next project, please contact us below. Join us in redefining the visual standards of the fashion industry.
